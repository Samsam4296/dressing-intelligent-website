How to delete your account

You can delete your Attira account at any time. Deleting your account will permanently erase all your data.

Option 1: From the app

  1. Open the Attira app
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Scroll down to "Delete my account"
  4. Confirm the deletion

Option 2: By email

Send an email to erreurprixfr@gmail.com with:

  • Subject: "Account deletion request"
  • The email address associated with your Attira account

We will process your request within 30 days.

Warning: Account deletion is irreversible. All of the following data will be permanently deleted:
  • Your profile and personal information
  • All your clothing photos
  • Your profiles (family)
  • Your outfit history
  • Your preferences and settings
