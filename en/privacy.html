1. Introduction

Dressing Intelligent ("we", "our", "the App") is published by NAIDJI SAMIR, a sole proprietorship registered in France. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This privacy policy describes the information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Data You Provide

  • Account information: email address and display name during registration.
  • Clothing photos: images you take or import to catalog your wardrobe.
  • Profiles: names and preferences of profiles you create (e.g., family members).

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Usage data: interactions with the app, features used, timestamps.
  • Technical data: device type, OS version, anonymous identifiers for crash reports (via Sentry).

2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your precise location (GPS).
  • We do not sell any personal data to third parties.
  • We do not use your photos for advertising purposes.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively to:

  • Provide and improve the app's features.
  • Generate personalized outfit suggestions.
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve stability.
  • Send you relevant notifications (if you've enabled them).

4. Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored on Supabase servers (hosted in the European Union). Photos are stored on Cloudinary with restricted access. All communications are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS.

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: authentication and database.
  • Cloudinary: image storage and processing.
  • Sentry: error and performance monitoring.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: push notifications.

Each of these services has its own privacy policy.

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • Access: obtain a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectification: correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: request the deletion of your account and data.
  • Portability: receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: object to the processing of your data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: erreurprixfr@gmail.com

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all your personal data and photos are deleted within 30 days.

8. Children

The app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors under 13.

9. Changes

We may update this privacy policy. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. In case of significant changes, we will notify you via the app.
